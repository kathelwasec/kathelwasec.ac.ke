HISTORY & BACKGROUND

Kathelwa Mixed Day Secondary School is a public subcounty category of school which is in the range of over 7000 such secondary schools in Kenya. It is situated in Kathelwa Sublocation, Akiriang'ondu location in Igembe central in Meru County. Geographically it is located west of Nairobi longitude 90o East and latitude 0.31o North.The area is a moderately warm environment with peasantly economic activities and a relative moderate population status. It is 5Km along Kangeta-Laare road and bare 10 minutes drive from both towns either.

It is sponsered by MCK in Kenya having started in 2006 initiated by community from the idea of microplanning unit where the first cash for starting the school had been primarily earmarked for society Sacco group. The enrolment was paltry 39 students on the onset and later the number grew to 73 when the first government Principal was posted in the year 2008 (March).Since the school has expanded to the current status of a 3-streamed school. Teachers on duty are currently 22 TSC teachers and 6 BOM teachers and 13 Non-teaching members of staff across all cadre.

There is bright reflection of school Alumni where nearly every year the Boys and Girls who have done well in KCSE Exams have joined Tertiary Institutions with the performance distributed from 2009 to date.The school infrastructure has also been expansive from the first class. Currently the institution is managed by a vibrant BOM under the able leadership of Mr. George Ntombiro Miriti who took over from the year 2019 to the date of this Web Launch.

The PA chair is Madam Rose Ntongai who is very committed and mindful of the wellness of the learners. Finally the sponsor(MCK) has played a very positive role in nurturing spiritual talents. Special thanks goes to the futive sister school Kathelwa Primary School, the futive Kathelwa community leaders led by Chief Joshua K. Gideon and his Assistant Peter Gitonga, former MCAs and MPs and most withstanding Hon Daniel Kiili Karitho who has really boosted the school image by awarding bursary to students in a very unique way and above that promising to have a school Bus funded by NG-CDF during his first term in office.

The good teachers, students, parents, non-teaching staff, BOM members, PTA and the entire well wishers fraternity, you are all highly valued by us. The future of Kathelwa is bright and solid for all is possible with God.
